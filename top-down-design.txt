#-------------------------#
# Task: Making a Sandwich #
#-------------------------#

Get Supplies
  Go to cupboard
    Grab knife
    Grab cutting board
  Go to counter
    Put supplies down

Get Ingredients
  Go to fridge
    Grab turkey
    Grab lettuce
    Grab cheese
  Go to pantry
    Grab bread


Assemble Sandwich
  Arrange bread on cutting board
  Assemble top half
    Add cheese
      Slice cheese
        Put cheese on cutting board
        Slice cheese with knife
      Put cheese on one slice of bread
    Add turkey
      Open package
      Put on top of cheese
  Assemble bottom half
    Put lettuce on bread
  Put the two halves together
